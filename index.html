Our Global Campus

In a simple but impressive function, first brick laying ceremony of the new campus of the University of Swabi was inaugurated. The inauguration ceremony was performed by Prof. Dr. Noor Jehan, Vice Chancellor of the University of Swabi. Amongst the participants, besides senior faculties and Heads of Departments, notables and elites of the area attended the ceremony. Speaking on the occasion Prof. Dr. Noor Jehan highlighted the importance of advanced and quality education towards advancement of the area in all fields in general and the education sector in particular.
